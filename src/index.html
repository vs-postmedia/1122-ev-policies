Percentage of vehicles registered with ICBC that are electric as of Sept. 30, 2023, by postal code.

  • The V6J postal code has the highest rate of electric vehicle ownership in the province. In 2022, there were 2,287 Teslas in this postal code – the vast majority of registered EVs.

  • West Vancouver has the second-highest rate of electric vehicle ownership in the province.

  • Ownership rates in the southern tip of Vancouver Island range from about three to seven per cent.

  • The in the Okanagan, around two per cent of registered vehicles are electric.

  • EV ownership rates in rural and remote parts of B.C. remains extremely low – typically under one per cent.
